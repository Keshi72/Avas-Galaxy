Saturday
Walk Day
30–45 minutes · the fat-burning backbone of this whole plan
Walk
Walking in a fasted or semi-fasted state (morning before a big meal, or 2+ hours after eating) at a comfortable pace is one of the most effective fat-burning tools available. It doesn't feel like exercise. It feels like living. That's the point.
Get dressed and out the door
The only hard part
Walk for at least 30 minutes
Comfortable pace — you should be able to talk
Notice something beautiful
This is joy, not exercise
🌿 Saturday walk is joy practice AND your main cardio. Put on something you love, a playlist that moves you, and go somewhere that feels good. You're not exercising — you're living beautifully.
Eventually — add a second walk on Wednesday mornings before Flow & Flex. Two walks a week plus pilates is a genuinely powerful combination for your goals.
