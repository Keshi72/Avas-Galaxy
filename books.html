THE LIBRARY

a moon season archive · ideas · language · connection · glimpses

I read without images — no pictures form behind my eyes. What I have instead is something purer: the rhythm of language, the weight of an idea, the feeling of understanding something I had always known but never said. Sometimes, in the rarest sentences, a glimpse arrives. Those are the ones I keep.
