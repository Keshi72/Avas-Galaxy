SHOE TO HEM RULE
Match shoe color to hem tone. Nude under cream. Tan under linen. White under white. Same family — creates an unbroken line that adds height.
Terracotta dress → tan or rust sandal. Sage outfit → nude or sage shoe. Dusty blue → silver, nude, or white.
When in doubt — nude or tan works with everything in your palette.

STYLIST BLOCK BREAKERS
One statement, one neutral. Dress is loud → shoes and bag quiet. Bag is the moment → dress simple.
When nothing feels right — reach for cream or linen. Your most neutral palette always works. Add one color piece.
Start with the bottom. Pick the skirt or dress first. Everything else responds to it.
The outfit formula: waist-defining piece → matching shoe tone → one gold jewelry → natural bag → one hair piece if needed.
