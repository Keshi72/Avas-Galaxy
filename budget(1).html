YOUR FINANCIAL RULES
SAVINGS LEAVE FIRST
$700 transferred the day you get paid. Before anything else.
BUFFER BEFORE CAR FUND
Get $500 sitting untouched before the car fund builds. One unexpected expense without a buffer is a crisis. With $500 it's an inconvenience.
48 HOURS BEFORE BUYING
Close the tab. If you're still thinking about it in 48 hours it's worth buying. If you forgot about it, you have your answer.
DON'T UPGRADE WHAT WORKS
If it works and it's cheap, leave it alone. The upgrade instinct costs more than the upgrade is worth.
THE PARKING LOT
Screenshot things you want and save them. Review once a month. Most won't feel urgent anymore. The ones that still do — those are worth buying.
CONTRACT REALITY
Ask your recruiter the review timeline. Until then execute the plan. The contract decision is outside your control — your savings rate is not.
GOAL ORIENTED ONLY
Before every purchase ask: does this move me toward my goals or away from them? Car, license, stability, radiology, Austin. Everything else waits.
