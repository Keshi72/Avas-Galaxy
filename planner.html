🧠 AUDHD Ground Rules — Read These First
One thing at a time. This planner has a lot in it. That doesn't mean you do it all at once. Pick ONE focus for today and do that. Everything else waits.

Done is better than perfect. A 20-minute study session that actually happened beats a 2-hour one you planned but didn't. Lower the bar. Show up. That's it.

When you spiral, use the Three Question Reset: What am I actually trying to do right now? What's the smallest possible step? Can I do just that one thing?

Stimulation budget. You get a finite amount of focus per day. Protect mornings. Don't open social media before you've done one goal task. The scroll can wait.

Progress is not linear. A bad week doesn't erase a good month. Keep going.

🧹 AUDHD + Cleaning — The Only System That Works
Don't clean the whole house. Pick one room, one task per day. Monday: kitchen. Tuesday: bathroom. Wednesday: bedroom. Thursday: living space. Friday: laundry. That's it. Five small tasks. Never a mountain.

The 2-minute rule: If it takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately. Don't put it down, put it away.

Sunday reset (20 minutes): Quick tidy of all rooms, prep meals, lay out clothes for the week. This one habit changes how Monday feels.

YOUR DAILY SCHEDULE
DAILYtemplate
Your AUDHD-Friendly Daily Structure
This is a framework, not a prison. The goal is that the structure removes the need to decide — you just follow the next step. On hard days, even doing 3 of these is a win.
WEEKDAY — Before School Starts
7:30
Wake up
Water first. Sit by a window for 5 min. No phone yet. This is not negotiable.
7:45
Breakfast
Something with protein. Don't skip it — your focus depends on it.
8:30
Khan Academy — 30 min
Same spot, same time every day. Streak beats output. Just sit down and start.
9:00
Admin / Money — 30 min
One task only: apply for job, check loan, update budget, email office. One thing.
10:00
Walk — 20–30 min
Outside if possible. This is your movement and your mental reset. Both at once.
11:00
Free / Creative time
Sketchbook, crochet, music, reading — anything that isn't productive on purpose.
12:30
Lunch
Sit down to eat. Away from screens if you can manage it.
1:30
Rest / Decompress
Actual rest. A show, music, nap. Zero productivity required. This refills you.
3:30
Second study or admin block — 45 min
Medical terminology, driver's handbook, or the next admin task. One thing.
5:30
Dinner
Something you meal prepped if possible. This is where the Kitchen planner saves you.
7:00
Wind down — no new tasks
Show, music, crochet. The day is done. Your brain needs to power down.
9:30
No new screens / prep tomorrow
Dim lights. Lay out what you need. Write one intention for tomorrow in The Diary.
10:30
Lights out
7+ hours. Sleep is medication for AUDHD. Protect it like your most important job.
WEEKDAY — During School
7:00
Wake up — earlier now
Water. 5 minutes quiet. Do not scroll yet. Get dressed before you sit down.
7:30
Breakfast + pack bag
Bring water, snacks, any notes you need. Leave 10 minutes early — always.
—
School / Class
Sit near the front. Take handwritten notes — it helps AUDHD retain information better.
Between
Movement between classes
Walk outside for 5 minutes between classes. Don't sit on your phone — decompress physically.
After class
Decompression — 30 min minimum
You've been regulated all day. You need to decompress before you can study again.
+1 hr
Study block — 45 min max
Pomodoro: 25 min on, 5 min break, 25 min on. Timer is mandatory. Stop when it rings.
Eve
Dinner + rest
You worked hard today. Eat well. Rest fully. Tomorrow you do it again.
9:30
Prep tomorrow + lights down
One intention. What do you need to bring? What's the one thing tomorrow needs from you?
🧠 When You Hit a Wall
You will have bad days. Days where nothing gets done and you spiral for three hours on something unrelated to your goals. That's okay. It's part of having AUDHD.

When it happens:
1. Stop. Go outside for 5 minutes.
2. Do one tiny thing from your list — not the big one, the tiny one.
3. Write in The Diary what happened. Don't judge it. Just record it.
4. Sleep on it. Tomorrow is not today.

You are not behind. You are building.
