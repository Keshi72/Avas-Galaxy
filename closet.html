Fabric Care Guide

Your wardrobe is natural fabrics only. Treat them right and they last years.

Linen
·Cold wash, gentle cycle only
·Lay flat or hang to dry — never tumble
·Iron while slightly damp on medium heat
·Softens beautifully with every wash
·Never use bleach — yellows the fibres
Organic Cotton
·30°C wash, gentle cycle
·Tumble dry low or air dry
·Iron on medium — cotton can handle heat
·Wash inside out to preserve prints
·Wash with similar colours
TENCEL / Lyocell
·Cold wash, delicate cycle always
·Air dry — heat destroys the fibres
·Never wring or twist — press gently
·Steam rather than iron where possible
·Softens and drapes more beautifully over time
Merino Wool
·Hand wash cold or wool cycle only
·Lay flat to dry — never hang (stretches)
·No tumble dryer ever — will felt and shrink
·Wool wash detergent only (Eucalan or Woolite)
·Air between wears — self-cleaning fibres
Silk or Satin
·Hand wash cold in gentle detergent
·Never wring — roll in a towel to absorb water
·Air dry away from direct sunlight
·Iron on low with a pressing cloth between
·Store folded in cotton, not plastic
Velvet
·Spot clean where possible — avoid full wash
·If washing: cold, delicate, inside out
·Never iron directly — steam only
·Hang to dry — never fold (crushes pile)
·Brush pile gently when dry with a soft brush

A wardrobe built slowly and with intention is worth more than a wardrobe built fast. 🌿
